Data Entry
Dhaka, Bangladesh Part Time 5000 BDT

Job description

As a Data Entry Operator at Oceantec, you will play a crucial role in ensuring the accuracy and efficiency of our data management processes. This is a contract position where your salary will be calculated based on the number of days you fulfill the daily targets. A minimum typing speed of 36 words per minute is required.

Responsibility

  • - Accurate and timely data entry into our systems, including Google Sheets and Microsoft Word.
  • - Meet daily targets to qualify for compensation.
  • - Collaborate with team members to ensure data integrity.
  • - Maintain confidentiality and security of sensitive information.

Qualifications

  • - Minimum typing speed of 36 words per minute.
  • - Proficient in data entry software, Google Sheets, and Microsoft Office Suite.
  • - Attention to detail and accuracy in data entry.
  • - Excellent time management skills.
  • - Strong communication skills.
  • - Proven experience as a Data Entry Operator or similar role.
  • - Ability to work independently and meet deadlines.

Apply For The Job

If you are a detail-oriented and motivated individual with the required expertise, please submit your resume and cover letter to hr.oceantec@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Entry Operator Application - [Your Name]".
Be part of Oceantec's commitment to excellence and efficiency in data management.
